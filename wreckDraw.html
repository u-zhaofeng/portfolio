Wrecking Draw

I had thought of a game idea and simply couldn't let it go to waste. Completing it definitely made the months of work worth it though!

Project Overview

I was always interested in the game development process, as a gamer myself. Therefore, when I randomly thought of a good game idea, I saw it as a good opportunity to expand my knowledge in that direction. Given that I had internship during the day, I worked on this during my free time and after about 3 months of work, I completed this project and published it on itch.io making it all worth it in the end.

Through this experience, I learnt a lot about all aspects of game development. I saw this as more of a coding challenge though, and felt that my coding and problem solving skills have definitely improved.
